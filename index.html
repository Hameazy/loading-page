Intro

I am an Egyptian Palestinian born in America, bringing a diverse cultural perspective to my journey in technology. With a background that includes five years as a soccer referee and experience as a service associate at Kohl's, I have honed my skills in communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. I also served as a California Scholarship Federation tutor, where I developed a passion for teaching and mentoring. Check out my Linkedin here!.

Currently, I am a second-year Computer Science student at Sacramento State University, where I also work as a Student Assistant at the Legislative Data Center. My introduction to programming began in high school with an AP Computer Science Principles course, sparking a deep interest in technology and coding. During my freshman year at Sac State, I expanded my knowledge through various introductory coding classes and participated in the Web Development 101 CodePath program.

Projects

I have undertaken several impactful projects that showcase my skills and passion for technology. In March 2022, I developed the Country Statistics Application, a user-friendly tool that provides essential information about countries, including population, capital, and geographical data. This app, designed with offline functionality, is particularly useful for travelers and users in areas with limited connectivity. Following this, in February 2022, I created the American States Application, an educational resource offering comprehensive insights into U.S. states. This app not only ensures data accuracy but also includes a "Fun Facts" section, making it an engaging learning tool for users of all ages.

In March 2024, I launched the Movie Database, a robust platform allowing users to explore detailed movie information and manage their own entries. This project highlights my focus on user experience, featuring intuitive navigation and efficient search functionalities. Most recently, in May 2024, I developed an AI Chess Game that challenges players with varying difficulty levels. This game utilizes advanced algorithms to enhance gameplay, and includes features like move suggestions and game history tracking, offering both an educational and entertaining experience. These projects reflect my commitment to creating innovative and user-centric solutions, and I am eager to bring this same enthusiasm and expertise to new challenges in the tech industry.
